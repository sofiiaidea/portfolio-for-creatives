Local CORNER

Busy? Unsatisfied? Is life throwing crazy at you? And now you are going to have yet another sandwich from sketchy ingredients gathered from across the world? Depressing, right? No need to panic and go full recluse into the mountains. Just imagine, if the ingredients in that sandwich were from your corner...

What if you could try a Local Corner instead, where ingredients come from dedicated farmers from around BC, resulting in comforting, fresh, seasonal, organic and nutrient-dense food?

Sounds good, and we know you know it because it is food which will nourish you and make you feel like you have finally discovered a missing slice of home...
