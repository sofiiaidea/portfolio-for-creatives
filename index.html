  • About Me

    Hi, my name is Sofiia Kozenko and I am a third-year student at Capilano University’s IDEA School of Design focusing on Branding. My work is a constant pursuit of meaning; I believe that truly remarkable and useful things seed from it. Lack of meaning vanishes the purpose, heading into the oblivion of directionless and senseless. My aspiration is to masterfully orchestrate the viewer's engagement with the core of meaning that defines the essence of my creative opus. I direct my attention toward photography, product designs and campaigns, innovative user experiences, unique interfaces, and in-depth creative thinking.
